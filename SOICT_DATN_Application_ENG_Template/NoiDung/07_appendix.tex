\section*{Use case: Record Customer Payment}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Actor:} Accountant or Administrator.
  \item \textbf{Pre-conditions:} Sales invoice exists; invoice status allows
        payment; payment amount is greater than zero and does not exceed the
        remaining amount.
  \item \textbf{Main flow:} actor opens invoice detail $\to$ enters payment
        amount, payment method, and payment reference $\to$ system validates the
        amount $\to$ system updates paid amount and remaining amount $\to$
        invoice status becomes PARTIALLY\_PAID or PAID $\to$ customer balance
        and sales order payment status are recalculated.
  \item \textbf{Alternate flow:} payment amount is invalid or exceeds remaining
        amount $\to$ system rejects the operation and keeps invoice unchanged.
  \item \textbf{Post-conditions:} payment information is recorded; invoice and
        related sales order payment statuses are updated.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Use case: Adjust Stock}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Actor:} Warehouse Staff or Administrator.
  \item \textbf{Pre-conditions:} Product and warehouse exist; actor has
        warehouse permission; adjustment reason is provided.
  \item \textbf{Main flow:} actor selects inventory record $\to$ enters the
        adjustment quantity and reason $\to$ system validates the quantity $\to$
        system updates on-hand and available quantity $\to$ system creates an
        inventory transaction for audit.
  \item \textbf{Alternate flow:} adjustment would make stock negative $\to$
        system rejects the operation; actor lacks permission $\to$ system
        rejects the request.
  \item \textbf{Post-conditions:} inventory quantity reflects the adjustment and
        an audit transaction is stored.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Use case: Monitor Expiring Lots}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Actor:} Warehouse Staff, Sales Staff, Sales Manager, or
        Administrator.
  \item \textbf{Pre-conditions:} Inventory lots exist with expiry-date
        information.
  \item \textbf{Main flow:} actor opens the inventory lot list $\to$ selects a
        product, warehouse, or expiry filter $\to$ system returns lots sorted by
        FEFO $\to$ actor views lot number, remaining quantity, expiry date,
        days until expiry, and status.
  \item \textbf{Alternate flow:} actor opens expiring-soon view $\to$ system
        lists lots whose expiry date is within the selected threshold; actor
        opens expired view $\to$ system lists expired lots with remaining stock.
  \item \textbf{Post-conditions:} actor obtains information for replenishment,
        promotion, return, or disposal decisions; no stock quantity is changed.
\end{itemize}
